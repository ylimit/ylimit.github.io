I’m a Ph.D student in the Institute of Software at Peking University, advised by Yao Guo, Xiangqun Chen and Gang Huang. I was also advised by Jason Hong and Yuvraj Agarwal when I was a visiting student in the Human-Computer Interaction Institute at Carnegie Mellon University. My research interests lie in the fields of mobile computing, security and privacy, and software engineering.
